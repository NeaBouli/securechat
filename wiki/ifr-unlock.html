IFR Token Lifetime Unlock

Lock once. Unlock forever. No subscriptions.

SecureChat uses the same IFR Token system as SecureCall and Chameleon. Lock IFR tokens in the on-chain contract — while locked, you have lifetime access to premium features. Unlock your tokens any time, no penalties.
