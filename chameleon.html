How It Works

Privacy on autopilot.

Set it up once. Then forget about it. Chameleon does the rest.

🦎

Like a bodyguard that never sleeps.

Imagine a bodyguard who stands quietly behind you. You don't notice them. Nobody else does either.

But the moment something doesn't look right — they step in. Automatically. Silently.

That's Chameleon. You tell it once: “Protect me when I'm outside. Protect me when I'm on a strange WiFi. Protect me when I open my messages.”

And then you forget about it. It watches. It adapts. It protects. Without you doing anything.

3 simple steps.

⚙️

Tell it your rules. Once.

Open the app and set simple rules. “When I'm on public WiFi → protect everything.” “When I open my messages → hide the content.” “When I leave home → switch on full protection.” That's it. You're done.

👁️

It watches quietly in the background.

Chameleon runs silently on your phone. It notices which app you open. Which WiFi you're on. Where you are. Everything stays on your phone. Nothing is sent anywhere.

🛡️

Protection turns on by itself.

The moment one of your rules applies — Chameleon acts. Instantly. You don't press a button. You don't have to remember anything. It just happens.

Capabilities

Context-aware privacy, zero effort.

Chameleon adapts your privacy level automatically. No manual toggles. No forgotten settings. Just protection that follows you.

🔒

Overlay Encryption

Encrypts text in ANY app via Android AccessibilityService. Type normally — Chameleon encrypts before the text leaves your keyboard. Works with every messenger, email, and note app.

⚙️

Rule-Based Automation

Define encryption rules by WiFi network, GPS location, or active app. Connect to public WiFi? Encryption activates. Open a specific app? Privacy mode engages. Zero manual steps.

🗄️

Private Zone

Encrypted file storage powered by XChaCha20-Poly1305. Photos, documents, notes — everything locked behind military-grade encryption. Accessible only with your master key.

🎭

Decoy Profile

Enter the wrong PIN and Chameleon activates an empty decoy profile. No contacts, no messages, no files. The real data stays invisible and encrypted beneath the surface.

📍

Geofencing

Privacy level changes automatically based on your physical location. At home? Relaxed mode. At work or traveling? Maximum encryption. Define zones once, forget forever.

🔑

IFR Token Access

Same IFR token lock as SecureChat. One on-chain lock unlocks lifetime access to both Chameleon and SecureChat. No subscriptions, no renewals.
